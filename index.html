\ AMERICAN PIT BULL TERRIER

The Unsung Story of America's Favorite Dog

A Mixed Breed dog, made up from the combinations of An English Terrier and Various Bull Dog Breeds. It was originally bred to assist in bull-bating but then pit bulls were used in dog fighting back in the 1800's in England. Once the pit bull came to America in the late 1800's it took on more of a domesticated role, but maintained the stigma of aggressive and fiesty. In all truth, Pit Bulls are actually very friendly dogs, often maintaining the treatment OF it's owner/handler. In General, Pit Bull dogs are very loving and keen to protection of its loved ones.
